What is Visual Snow Syndrome?

Visual Snow Syndrome (VSS) is a neurological condition where people see static, flickering dots across their vision, even in complete darkness.

Extension Features

Our browser extension helps simulate VSS effects for research and awareness. Features include static overlays, flickering effects, and adjustable intensity settings.

Support & Awareness

Raising awareness is crucial! Learn more from medical sources and share your experience with VSS communities.
